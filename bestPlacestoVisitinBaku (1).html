Best Places to Visit in Baku

Baku, the capital city of Azerbaijan, is a vibrant destination that seamlessly blends ancient history with modernity. Here are some of the best places to visit in Baku:

  • Icherisheher

    • Icherisher, also known as the Old City, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is home to historical landmarks such as the Maiden Tower and the Palace of the Shirvanshahs. The narrow cobblestone streets, ancient architecture, and vibrant bazaars make it a must-visit destination.


  • Flame Towers

    • The Flame Towers are iconic skyscrapers that dominate Baku's skyline. These modern structures are designed to resemble flames, symbolizing Azerbaijan's rich history with fire. At night, the towers are illuminated with LED lights, creating a stunning visual display.


  • Haydar Aliyev Center

    • The Haydar Aliyev Center is an architectural masterpiece designed by Zaha Hadid. Its flowing, curved design stands out in contrast to the city's more traditional architecture. The center hosts exhibitions, cultural events, and conferences, making it a hub for art and culture in Baku.
